Under the mission "Turn water into a better world", Fluidra is a major player in the swimming pool manufacturing industry with strong values and a great work culture. I had the chance to work surrounded by amazing professionals in the Iberia Marketing team, building brand and B2B customer experience from their headquarters in Sant Cugat del Vallès, near Barcelona.

At Fluidra, we operate under a range of different commercial brands that each cater to specific market needs within the pool and wellness industry. This brand architecture allows us to stay close to our customers and deliver specialised content through each channel.

Over the past few months, we've seen significant growth in one of our key Instagram accounts — reaching nearly 20,000 followers in just three months. This rapid increase reflects the relevance of our content strategy, community engagement, and consistency in visual storytelling.

As part of the content team, I focused on designing infographics and branded visuals for Instagram posts that required graphic design support — boosting visibility, encouraging saves and shares, and reinforcing each brand's positioning as a trusted source of knowledge and innovation in the pool industry.

Context
Multi-brand architecture in a global manufacturing leader

Fluidra operates multiple commercial brands under one roof — each with distinct audiences, visual identities and content needs. The challenge: maintain brand coherence across the whole portfolio while giving each brand its own distinct voice and aesthetic.

Approach
Content strategy meets visual craft

Developed branded infographics and visual content for Instagram with a strong focus on clarity, aesthetics and alignment with each brand's visual identity. Every design had to work both as a standalone post and as part of a cohesive feed strategy.

Outcome
20,000 followers in 3 months

One of the key Instagram accounts grew to nearly 20,000 followers in just three months — a direct result of consistent, high-quality branded content. Posts optimised for saves, shares and engagement reinforced positioning as an industry authority.
