During my internship at English for Fashion, I explored how design and education can connect through storytelling, culture, and visual identity. The brand helps fashion professionals and creatives improve their communication skills in English — and my role was to translate that mission into a distinctive visual language across digital channels.

I worked closely with the founder to design social media campaigns, course visuals, and newsletter layouts that reflected the sophistication and creativity of the fashion industry. I introduced a more consistent use of typography, colour, and visual rhythm to align the educational content with the tone of international fashion communication.

This experience allowed me to merge my background in graphic design, branding, and digital marketing with a new understanding of how education and aesthetics can work together. I focused on creating visuals that were not only visually appealing but also clear, structured, and pedagogical — helping learners engage more intuitively with the brand's content.

It was a small, dynamic environment where I could experiment freely, test creative ideas quickly, and see immediate impact — reinforcing my interest in design systems that make information intuitive, inspiring, and accessible.

Context
Fashion education meets editorial visual language

English for Fashion occupies a niche intersection — premium language education for fashion professionals. The visual identity needed to feel as sophisticated as the industry it served, while remaining clear and pedagogical enough to support learning.

Approach
Typography, colour and visual rhythm as tools

Introduced consistent typographic systems, colour language and visual rhythm across all digital touchpoints. Drew from fashion editorial references — runway aesthetics, magazine layouts — to create content that felt native to the industry the brand served.

Outcome
A cohesive digital presence across all channels

Delivered a unified visual system for social media campaigns, course visuals and newsletters — giving the brand a consistent, elevated presence that matched the tone and ambition of the international fashion education market.
